XY Sprite Project

A collection of sprites by Smogon and Deviantart artists for use on smogon.com
and Pokemon Showdown.

When using these sprites outside of Smogon or Pokemon Showdown be mindful to
credit the artists who made the original sprite, and the XY Sprite Project on
Smogon. This project was a communal effort and everyone involved deserves their
proper thanks.

Mini icons collected by ium.
