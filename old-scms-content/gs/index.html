The G/S generation began in 2000 following the North American release of Pokémon Gold and Silver. It introduced 100 new Pokémon, the split of Special (Spc) into Special Attack (SpA) and Special Defense (SpD), items, and two new types, Steel and Dark. G/S is played online on the simulator NetBattle.
