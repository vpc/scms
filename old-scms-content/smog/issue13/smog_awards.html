Here it is ladies and gentlemen, your Smogon Awards 2010 nominees. After sorting through the mass of votes cast it was whittled down to the top 5 nominees in each category. When casting your vote for each category be sure to only vote for one (1) nominee. Send in your ballots via PM to The Smog. Good luck to all the nominees and if you have any questions direct them to The Smog and we will be sure to answer them in a timely fashion.

The deadline for casting your votes is before the release of Issue 14 of The Smog, so cast your ballot early!
